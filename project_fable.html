A Fable Tale

This project was made by our 7-person team, for our Game World Design class at ITU.
Besides composing the soundtrack, I mainly worked as a 2D animator and concept artist on the art department.

The game's protagonist is Theo, a little mink with magical powers.
This is an early pencil test I animated during the early stages of development, when it was still a 2D side-scroller
(character design was drawn by our art lead).

Later on, we decided to go with a 2.5D approach instead.
For this to work, I created a 45 degree version of Theo's test animation (once again based off of our art lead's drawing),
and modeled a 3D reference to use as guide for the level designers and our background artist.

Theo's friend is a crow named Roo. He was planned to follow Theo around, helping them in puzzles.
Here's the first animation pencil test.

These are some concepts I drew for the game's antagonist, the trickster raccoon.
Later on he was scrapped from the game for narrative reasons.

Here's another scrapped NPC, the lizard merchant.
He was a fun character to draw and ideate on.

And these are two ideas we tossed around in the beginning. The original protagonist was supposed to be a human monk.
And the antagonist was an ominous embodiment of nightmares.
